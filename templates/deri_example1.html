Derivative Exmaples

    Example 1: \(y = x^4 + 6x^3\)

    Solution:

    \(\;\;\;\;\;\dfrac{dy}{dx} = \dfrac{d}{dx}(x^4) + 6\cdot \dfrac{d}{dx}(x^3)\)

    \(⇒ \dfrac{dy}{dx} = 4x^{4-1} + 6 \times 3 (x^{3-1})\)

    \(⇒ \dfrac{dy}{dx} = 4x^3 + 18x^2\)


    Example 2: \(y = x^3 + 8x^2 - 2x\)

    Solution:

    \(\;\;\;\;\;\dfrac{dy}{dx} = \dfrac{d}{dx}(x^3) + 8\cdot \dfrac{d}{dx}(x^{2})- 2\cdot \dfrac{d}{dx}(x)\)

    \(⇒ \dfrac{dy}{dx} = 3x^{3-1} + 8 \times 2 (x^{2-1}) - 2\)

    \(⇒ \dfrac{dy}{dx} = 3x^2 + 16x - 2\)


    Example 3: \(y = 5x^4 + 3x^{-3}\)

    Solution:

    \(\;\;\;\;\;\dfrac{dy}{dx} = 5\cdot \dfrac{d}{dx}(x^4) + 3\cdot \dfrac{d}{dx}(x^{-3})\)

    \(⇒ \dfrac{dy}{dx} = 5 \times 4 x^{3-1} + 3 \times (-3) (x^{-3-1})\)

    \(⇒ \dfrac{dy}{dx} = 20x^2 - 9x^{-4}\)
